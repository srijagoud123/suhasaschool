ABOUT US

AT SUHASA we believe that Early Childhood Education is crucial because it marks a critical period of rapid brain development where experiences and opportunities lay the foundation for future learning, social skills, and overall well-being, impacting a child's trajectory throughout life; essentially, high-quality early childhood education can significantly improve cognitive, social, and emotional development, setting children up for success in school and beyond.

Our state-of-art SUHASA Day Care facility we provide your young children with a quality preschool experience in a safe and nurturing environment. A play-based, developmentally appropriate curriculum provides experience for academic and social growth.

SUHASA's Literacy-based Curriculum In Pre-Kindergarten and Kindergarten classrooms, the learning environment is the curriculum foundation. Classrooms are divided into well-defined learning areas that encourage exploration, work, and active learning through individual, small-group, and large-group activities.
Learning Centers include dramatic play, art, music, creative expression, construction, science, reading/language, writing/literacy and sensory exploration. Children are a part of the planning process, making suggestions for themes and topics to investigate.

SUHASA GOALS:>
• Provide meaningful developmentally appropriate learning experiences based on the individual developmental needs of each child
• Help children value and respect themselves and others
• Stimulate creativity and self-expression
• Promote language and literacy skills
• Stimulate cognitive development
• Promote health, physical growth, and motor development
• Encourage self-discipline
• Help children become accepting, caring individuals and develop supportive and helpful classroom relationships
